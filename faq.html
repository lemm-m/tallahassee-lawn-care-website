Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to your most common lawn care questions.

Pricing & Estimates

Yes, all our estimates are 100% free with no obligation. We typically provide quotes within 24 hours of your request, and for many standard properties, we can provide an estimate using satellite imagery to save you time.

Pricing depends on the size of your property, its current condition, and the frequency of service (weekly vs. bi-weekly). We offer competitive, transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Contact us for a precise quote tailored to your yard.

Scheduling & Contracts

No, we do not require long-term contracts for our residential customers. We believe our quality of work will keep you as a customer. We operate on a month-to-month basis, though commercial accounts can request annual contracts.

Lawn Maintenance

Our standard lawn maintenance package includes mowing all turf areas, line trimming (weed-eating) around obstacles, mechanical edging along driveways and sidewalks, and blowing all debris off hard surfaces.

In the event of heavy rain or severe weather, your service will be delayed. We typically shift our schedule back by one day to accommodate weather delays, and we will communicate any changes directly to you.
